My favorite movies of all time.

All the movies listed below are as of May 8, 2023, when I created this website.

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

This movie is so special to me because it is about a guy named Walter, who day dreams a lot because his life is so mundane. Then he goes on an adventure and the daydreaming gets less and less. The movie is about bravery and adventure, going into the unknown, which is the exact reason why I love it so much. It's like a life lesson.

The Big Year

This movie has similar concept as the movie above. Living life to the fullest and experiencing everything as you go. No regrets!
